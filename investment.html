Why Mwanga

What the service offers

The proposal positions Mwanga as a specialist provider of structured assessments with a focus on quality, access, and clear reporting.

Core Assessment Services

  • Comprehensive mental health assessments
  • Mental Status Examination (MSE)
  • Risk and safeguarding assessments
  • Capacity assessments
  • Trauma assessments
  • Crisis / urgent assessments

Specialist & High-Demand Services

  • PIP assessment support
  • Learning disability assessments
  • Fitness-for-work assessments
  • Medico-legal reports
  • Child & adolescent assessments
  • Second opinion assessments
Market Opportunity

Why this is a strong growth area

Service gap

Long NHS waiting lists and growing post-pandemic awareness continue to drive demand for faster access to mental health assessment services.

Referral demand

The proposal identifies demand from employers, legal professionals, benefit claimants, and families seeking quicker assessment pathways.

Local need

Blackpool and surrounding areas are identified as presenting strong opportunity because of service gaps and high levels of mental health need.
